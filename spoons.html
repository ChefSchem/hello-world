About Gold Spoons

The gold spoon (Dicula Auri) is native to homes or wealthy individuals thorughout Europe and North America. Its popularity status is currently Not Affordable.

There are many subspecies within the gold spoon species, including the Harold Import and the BRILA Stainless Steel.

Features

Gold Spoons are not always completely gold. Some can rust, fade, or just be a coating.They have very large, curved protrusions that is wonderful when comsuming soups or stews. Certified Gold Spoons are often 30% heavier than knockoff Gold Spoons. They can range from $20 to $420.
